I love Dungeons & Dragons

and you should too!


Dungeons & Dragons is a Tabletop Role Playing Game (TTRPG) where you can create cool characters with your friends and go on high fantasy adventures together! You could be a half-elf ranger with a grizzly bear companion. Or maybe a tiefling cleric with a penchant for mischief? Or perhaps a human monk who can deflect bullets is more your thing? Whatever your preference, there's something for you!

Go on an epic journey with a bunch of pals; be morally conscious or let chaos reign, the choice is up to you. Commence your quest to save the world, or die trying. Will you succeed? The dice will decide!

Before you start playing though, I recommend watching the hit series Critical Role on Youtube to get an immersive experience on how to get the most out of your game.
